# 🎧 Music Store Business Intelligence (SQL)

**The Project:** An exploratory data analysis of a digital music store's relational database, designed to translate raw invoice and catalog data into actionable business strategy. 

**The Approach:** Leveraging advanced SQL techniques—including CTEs, multi-table joins, and window functions—I queried customer and sales data to uncover key purchasing trends. The analysis targets three core areas:
* **Market Expansion:** Identifying the most profitable countries and cities for targeted marketing.
* **Catalog Strategy:** Pinpointing top-grossing artists and the most popular genres.
* **Customer Retention:** Highlighting high-value customers and top-performing sales representatives.

**The Code:** 📄 `music_sales_analysis.sql`
